Prime Supermarket

Overview

This project focused on creating a website to digitalize the business and provide an interactive, immersive online experience to enhance customer engagement while aligning with sustainable principles. In this group project, I developed customer-facing pages, including the login/sign-up pages, forgot password pages, and the "My Profile" page, where customers can update their particulars, delete their account, or access their transaction history.

Contributions

  • Designed and implemented secure login and account recovery features, including encryption for sensitive data.
  • Developed the "My Profile" page for customers to manage their accounts and view transaction history.
  • Collaborated on improving user experience and accessibility across customer-facing pages.

Tools Used

  • Python Flask
  • SQLAlchemy
  • HTML/CSS
